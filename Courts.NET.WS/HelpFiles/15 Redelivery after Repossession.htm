15 Redelivery after Repossession

Any repossessed items can be redelivered by entering the account number in the redelivery after repossession screen.

The steps are:
  1. Choose the redelivery date
  2. Identify if this is for immediate delivery
  3. Choose a delivery area
  4. Mark for Redelivery
  5. Enter orders for delivery and collection
  6. Search for the order (please note that to find orders to be redelivered after repossession, the ‘Collection’ parameter must be ticked. Not the delivery parameter.
  7. Process the delivery as per normal
  8. Please note that warranties can never be redelivered after repossession.
