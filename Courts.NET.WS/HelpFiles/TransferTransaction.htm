Transfer Transactions

This allows transfer of money from one account to another. The options to transfer are:

• one customer account to another customer account (Code XFR)

• a customer account to the sundry credit account – typically when the customer has made a deposit payment (e.g. “dollar down”) – (Code SCX)

• the sundry credit account to the customer account (SCX) – the reversal of the above

• Transferring from the sundry credit account to journal (Code JLX)

If choosing the sundry credit account it will choose the sundry credit account belonging to the branch that you are currently in.

If transferring from the sundry credit account to journal the system will prompt you with a date. This is so that it can calculate the amount outstanding up to that date. For instance if you assume after 3 months the customer is unlikely to come back for a refund then you could choose to transfer to journal all transactions from more than 3 months ago. The system will sum up the sundry transactions prior to this date and then take into account any journal transactions made on this account. This will give you the correct amount you can transfer to journal. Setting the date to today’s date will transfer all sundry credits to the journal account.
