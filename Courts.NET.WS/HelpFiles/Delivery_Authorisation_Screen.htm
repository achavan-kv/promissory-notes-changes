Delivery Authorisation Screen

This screen is used to search for accounts that need to be delivery authorised before the delivery process can continue.

 

User rights

 

The following are the user rights which apply to the ‘Authorise Delivery’ screen:

 

  • Authorise Delivery: enables the user to have access to the ‘Authorise Delivery’ screen, enabling them to search for and view accounts that are awaiting to be delivery authorised.

  • Authroise Delivery – Load Other Branches: enables the branch drop down, giving the user the right to view accounts at a specific branch awaiting to be delivery authorised.

  • Authroise Delivery – Clear Proposal: Gives the user the right the clear the proposal from the screen.

 

Search parameters

 

The following are the search parameters used to determine which accounts are returned that are awaiting to be delivery authorised:

 

  • Include Cash – include and return Cash accounts from the search that are awaiting to be delivery authorised.

  • Include HP – include and return HP accounts from the search that are awaiting to be delivery authorised.

  • Include RF – include and return RF accounts from the search that are awaiting to be delivery authorised.

  • Deposit/Installment Paid – return accounts that have had their deposit/installment amount paid and are awaiting to be delivery authorised.

  • Deposit/Installment Not Paid – return accounts that have not had their deposit/installment paid and are awaiting to be delivery authorised.

  • Both – return accounts that have either had or have not had their deposit/installment paid and are awaiting to be delivery authorised.

  • Exclude Account With No Products – either tick or un-tick this to include or exclude accounts that have no products and are awaiting to be delivery authorised.

 

Delivery Authorising accounts

 

  • The search parameters should be used to search for the account you wish to clear from the Authorise Delivery screen.

  • Click on the ‘Search’ button, which should populate ‘Accounts Awaiting Clearance’.

  • You should be able to sort the accounts retrieved from the search by clicking on the columns.

  • Select the account from the ‘Accounts’ tab and proceed to clear any flags on the account from under the ‘Hold Flags’ grid by right-clicking on the flag and selecting the 'Clear Flag’ option.

  • To clear the proposal and delivery authorise the account, select the account from the ‘Accounts’ tab, right-click and select ‘Clear Proposal’.

  • After the account has been delivery authorised, it should now appear in the 'Orders for Delivery/Collection' screen.
