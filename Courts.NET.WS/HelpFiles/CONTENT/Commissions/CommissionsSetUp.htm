Setting up commissions

This screen will have two tab sections one for standard commissions and one for SPIFFS. The default tab selected will be the Standard Commission Area

 

Its important to understand the following definitions:

  • Product Category – Identified by the product category on stockitem table.
  • Product level – Identified by the first 3 digits of a product code.
  • Sku – This is the full product code.

The Commissions Tab

The standard commission area will be loaded with the Commission rates for the product category levels initially. There will be a drop-down which will have Product Category, Product Level, Product, and Termstypes. This will allow you to add commissions at each of these levels.

Note: Product Level overrides Product Category AND Product overrides Product Level. For example:

Category / Level or Product code) Commission
4 (Category) 10%
407 (Level) 6%
407306 (SKU) 8%

 

The commission earned on this item will be 8%

 

Loading Commissions

To see all the commissions that are or will be active on a certain date, choose the date and press reload.

Setting up commissions

At the bottom of the screen you will see the following fields:

  • Product Category / Level / Code (depending on which option is chosen from the drop down).
  • Commission %
  • Value (Only visible if terms type is chosen). The user must either put a % or a value in. Not both.
  • Date From (This is editable but may not be a date in the past)
  • Date to (This defaults to 2050 and is not editable. An end date for a commission will only be set when the commission is deleted OR a new commission rate is set up for that item)
  • There is a '+' button to add the new commission rate to the table.
  • There is a '-' button to delete and existing row in the commissions table.
