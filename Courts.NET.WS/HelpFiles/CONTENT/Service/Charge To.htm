Charge To Analysis

The ‘Charge To Analysis’ tab will appear next to the ‘Resolution’ tab. This will show a read only break down of the entities charged in the following table:

Costs Actual Costs Supplier Internal AIG Customer Deliverer
Parts - Courts            
Parts - Other            
Parts - Total            
Total Labour            
Total Cost            

 

The Price Index Matrix will be used to work out the costs. The Price Index holds monetary values in US$. An exchange rate for US$ must be set up. The charge to analysis tab will show values in local currency.

When the ‘primary charge to’ is the customer, the customer outstanding balance will be displayed on this tab.

When charging to a customer there may be a mark up applied to the labour and parts costs. This mark up is defined by the country parameters ‘SERVICE Labour Mark Up’ and ‘SERVICE Parts Mark Up’.

Additionally, the debtor’s cash account will have sales tax added.

Where AIG is charged for Parts, the total cost must be cost price of the part + 20%

When the ‘primary charge to’ is the deliverer, the user will be able to enter the deliverer’s name on this tab. Note that AIG can only be charged for an item sold on a Courts account with an Extended Warranty.
