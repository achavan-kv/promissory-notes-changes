7 Warranties on Credit

7.1 Setting up the ‘WC’ Termstype

This account will be assigned a termstype of WC (Warranty on Credit) in order to be able to identify a Cash/ Cash & Go Account were the Warranty was purchased on Credit. This termstype will need to be set up in termstype maintenance.

7.3 New Sales Order Process for Warranties on credit

 

Proceed with Cash or Cash & Go New Sales Order as per normal

If the item being purchased is associated with a warranty the Warranty Dialog Box will Pop Up.

Tick the checkbox, ‘buy on Credit’

The Customer Details Screen will automatically Launch to Allow customer details to be captured

NO CREDIT SANCTIONING will be invoked.

Once Customer details have been captured the user will go back to the New Sales Order screen and take the payment for the value of the items as usual (excl. warranty value)

7.4 Letters and Reports

Countries which use letters will have letters generated to inform the customer that they have x days to pay for the warranty otherwise the warranty will be terminated.

A further letter will be sent out at termination date to inform them they have a grace of x days to pay for the Warranty once again.

The Grace Letter will be sent out on the date the Pay Period expires

Warranty on Credit Reminder letter will be assigned the code: WCR .
