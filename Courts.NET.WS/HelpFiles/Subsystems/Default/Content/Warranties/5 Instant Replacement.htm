5 Instant Replacement

This section outlines the Instant Replacement process in COSACS. The areas covered in this section are:

 

  • Selling an Instant Replacement Warranty
  • Assessing the returned Item
  • Searching for the Item on the system

Please note that the Instant Replacement is a 2-year plan on Cash & Go items.

There are 3 Instant Replacement Periods. These are controlled by country parameters and can be found under the Instant replacement section of country maintenance:

 

  • Period 1 – Manufacturers Warranty
  • Period 2 – Courts Risk Period, courts bear the cost of the replacement and the original warranty stays in place
  • Period 3 – Courts risk period and the warranty is terminated

 

Any warranty that falls in the period after period 3 up until the end of the warranty life will be AIG risk and the warranty is terminated.
