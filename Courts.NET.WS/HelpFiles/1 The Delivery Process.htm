1 The Delivery Process

The process is:

 

  1. Create the sales order and indicate per line item if it is for immediate delivery or scheduling.
  2. Save and sanction as required
  3. Take Deposit or installment payments as required
  4. Authorize the delivery
  5. Load the account in the Orders for Delivery & Collection Screen
For Scheduled Orders
  1. Print Pick lists / Or print excel spreadsheet to list orders
  2. Schedule the delivery
  3. Print a Transport Pick list if required. (This is a Pick list of all scheduled Loads)
  4. Print the Delivery note and the Delivery schedule
  5. Notify the delivery
For Immediate delivery
  1. Print the Delivery notes
  2. Immediately delivery
